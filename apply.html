Application Details

To join Falcon Racing you must be a registered student at Seattle Pacific University and complete the following application. The application process runs from June through September each year and will include a formal cover letter and resume along with your application. Once your application materials have been reviewed you will be called in for an interview.

You can find the application here. Please email your application, cover letter, and resume to spufalconracing@gmail.com.
